About the Author

Vilate Jules Knapp was born in Lodi, California in 2004. She since moved to Utah, Montana/North Dakota, and currently resides in Idaho. Although young, she has great ambitions and wants to use her language to connect to people who feel lost. As a young girl she enjoyed music, and still does, incorporating music into her poetry and vice versa. She is studying to become a software engineer. She enjoys logic and critical thinking, so she takes great pleasure in problem solving.

Education

Vilate graduated from Fairview High School in 2022. She is currently working to receive her Bachelor's in Software Engineering from Brigham Young University - Idaho. She has always felt great joy from learning.

Work

Vilate has worked as an office cleaner, pizza artist, and - as an older sister of 4, as a nanny. She spent some time working in a warehouse. All of these jobs have helped her develop both soft and hard skills.
