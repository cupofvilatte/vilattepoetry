Glass

If you rise before the swallow,
If you need a Saint to follow,
Go down to the Seaside blue;
There the wide-waters swash,
A sanctuary for you to wash
Your heart as it carries you across
And into the refuge of a Lagoon.

The water there is crystal glass
And it purifies ache, and it cleanses ash
As well as the devoted soul
The Ocean waves crash wide
Hinting at the secrets it hides
In the coolness beneath its twirling tides

God and Child

Glory be to God for outstretched hands--
In His image this gaped mouth coos;
For a world by Him who set forth all;
He tumbles, he stumbles, this not-yet man;
Dismissed before he learns to tie his shoes;
By a world affected from the Fall.

Governments conspire against this babe;
And whatever else is pious, holy, good, too.
Listen careful for no-man knows when the call;
The youth of today will rise up and show the way:
Glory to Allah.

~~ Vilate
